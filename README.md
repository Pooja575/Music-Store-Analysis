# Music-Store-Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales and customer data from a digital music store (Chinook database) using SQL and Power BI.
The goal is to derive business insights related to revenue, customers, geography, and music preferences.

## Tools & Technologies

* SQL (PostgreSQL)
* Power BI
* Chinook Sample Database

## Business Questions Answered

* What is the total revenue and number of invoices?
* Who is the top customer by total spending?
* Which countries generate the highest revenue?
* What are the top-performing music genres?
* How many unique customers does the store have?

## Power BI Dashboard Highlights:

* KPI cards for revenue, invoices, customers, and top customer
* Top-N analysis for countries and genres
* Clean, business-friendly visual design

## SQL Analysis used to:

* Aggregate revenue and invoice data
* Identify top customers by spending
* Calculate genre and country-level revenue
* Prepare analysis-ready datasets for visualization
